A JOURNEY THROUGH GST

      GST (GOODS AND SERVICE TAX) IS AN INDIRECT TAX WHICH WAS INTRODUCED ON 1st JULY 2017 AND WHICH WAS APPLICABLE THROUGHOUT THE INDIA WHICH REPLACED MULTIPLE CASCADE LEVED TAXES BY THE CENTRAL AND STATE GOVERNMENT. IT WAS INTRODUCED AS THE 101 AMENDMENT ACT FOLLOWING THE PASSSAGE OF 122nd AMENDMENT BILL. GST IS GOVERNBED BY THE GST COUNCIL AND THE FINANCE MINISTER OF INDIA. UNDER GST, GOODS AND SERVICE ARE TAXED AT THE FOLLOWING RATES : 0%, 5%, 12%, 18%, 28%. THERE IS ROUGH RATE OF .25% ON PRECIOUS STONES AND GOLD. MAIN MOTTO OF GST WAS TO SHAPED THE 2 TRILLION DOLLAR ECONOMY OF INDIA.
