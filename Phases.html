HOW GST TOOK BIRTH

  • IN 2000, ATAL BIHARI VAJPAYEE FORMED A COMMITTEE FOR REVIEW ON GST UNDER THE CHAIRMANSHIP OF THEN FINANCE MINISTER ASIM DASGUPTA OF WESTBENGAL IN WHICH HE WAS GIVEN THE RESPONSIBILITY OF PREPARING THE GST MODEL

  • THE KELAKR TASK FORCE ADVISED UNITY INDIRECT TAXES IN THE FORM OF GST.

  • THE PROPOSED NATIONAL LEVEL GST IN APRIL 2010 WAS FIRST PLACED DURING THE 2006 BUDGET.

  • IN MAY 2017, AN EMPOWERED COMMITTEE OF FINANCE MINISTER WAS CONSTITUTED TO KNOW YHE IMPACT OF GST ON REVENUE OF DIFFERENT STATES OF INDIA AND IMPLEMENTATION OF GST

  • IN 2008-09 FINANCE MINISTER P CHIDAMBARAM HAD ANNOUNCED IN HIS SPEECH THAT GST WILL BE IMPLEMENTED FROM 2011

  • IN 2013, THE STANDING COMMITTEE PRESENTED ITS REPORT ON GST AND IN NOVEMBER 2009, THE PROPOSAL TO INCLUDE TO THE GOVERNMENT PETROLEUM PRODUCTS IN GST WAS REJECTED BY THE EMPOWERED COMMITTEE.

  • IN 2014, 122TH COMMISSION AMENDMENT WAS PASSED IN LOK SABHA

  • AFTER TWO YEARS OF WAITING RAJYA SABHA PASSED THE GST ON AUGUST 2, 2016 AND RECIEVE PRESIDENTS PRESIDENTS APPROVAL IN SEPTEMBER 2016

  • IN 2014, GOV INTRODUCED FOUR TYPES OF GST BILLS INCLUDING CENTRAL GST, INTEGRATED GST, GST BILL OF UNION TERRITORIES AND GST BILL

  • IN AUGUST 2016 WHEN ASSAM BECAME THE FIRST STATE TO PASS GST, THEN IN APRIL 2017, BIHAR BECAME THE FIRST NON-BJP RULED STATE TO PASS BILL

  • ON SEPTEMBER 23, 2016 GST NETWORK WAS FORMED, IT IS AN ONLINE NETWORK DESIGNED TO SOLVE THE PROBLEMS AND QUESTIONS OF CONSUMNERS AND BUISENESSMAN

  • ON MAY 20, THE GST COUNCIL FIXED FOR RATES OF GATES(5%, 12%, 18%, 25%)

  • THE LAUNCH OF GST WAS ANNOUNCED ON MAY, 2017

  • ON JUNE 20,2017 ARUN JAITLEY ANNOUNCED THAT GST WILL BE LAUNCHED ON THE LINES OF THE NIGHT OF INDEPENDENCE , OM THE MIDNIGHT OF JUNE 30..
